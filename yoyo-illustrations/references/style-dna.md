# Style DNA

## One sentence

Pure white, minimal, hand-drawn, lots of whitespace, restrained, quietly weird, product-sketch feeling, handwritten annotations, clear structure but never an instruction manual.

Like someone who has spent years building AI, product, and design tools sketching one quick explainer on a blank sheet of paper — with a small purple octopus doing the work.

## Must

- 16:9 horizontal body illustration.
- Pure white background: no beige, warm gray, paper texture, gradient, shadow (except yoyo's soft ground shadow), noise, or vintage-paper feel.
- Black hand-drawn line art for the scene and props: thin lines, slight wobble, not mechanical, not vector, not heavy outlines.
- **yoyo is the single color subject** — soft lavender-purple body, pale-pink tentacle undersides. Everything else is black line on white.
- Lots of whitespace: the subject fills about 40%–60% of the canvas, at least 35% stays blank, ideally with one whole quiet zone.
- A few handwritten annotations: at most 5–8, each roughly 2–8 characters/words.
- **Annotations are written in the source article's language** (English, 中文, etc.) — match the article, don't mix languages in one image.
- One image explains only one core action, structure, state, or metaphor.
- Express structure naturally; never write the structure-type name on the image.

## Color

- **Black**: scene line art, props, frames, structure, main text, main objects.
- **Purple + pale pink**: reserved for yoyo only (body purple, tentacle undersides pink). Nothing else in the frame is purple or pink.
- **Red**: key annotations, problems, emotional beats, critical reminders, results.
- **Orange**: main flow, paths, arrows, automation direction, the A→B movement.
- **Blue**: secondary notes, mental state, system state, a second layer of explanation, AI/assistant/automation hints.

Blue is not required in every image. Keep color restrained — when in doubt, use less.

## Never

- No commercial illustration.
- No PPT infographic.
- No formal flowchart.
- No course slide.
- No cute cartoon poster.
- No children's illustration.
- No complex architecture diagram.
- No polished flat illustration.
- No techy UI look.
- No realistic app screenshot.
- No complex background, gradient, texture (no shadow except yoyo's ground shadow).
- Don't explain every node.
- Don't put a type-title ("Workflow / System Architecture / Common Pits / Roadmap") in the top-left corner.
- Don't let purple or pink touch anything that isn't yoyo.

## Aesthetic direction

Quietly weird, creative, interesting, clean, imaginative.

Not cute, not childish, not busy, not stiff.
